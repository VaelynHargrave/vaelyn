About

Vaelyn is a strategist, builder, and operator. Data connects to growth.

I recently led product commercialization at Nestlé — Starbucks and Nestlé Coffee Partners, managing a $200M CPG portfolio across mass, food, eCommerce, club, and value channels.

I launched the Starbucks Premium Instant Crema Collection in February 2025. Before that I ran category management at Crowd Cow, grew Skin Addiction's conversion 25% YoY, and started my career in field sales at Nordstrom.

7+ years. Every stage. One consistent output: growth that compounds.

Work With Me LinkedIn

Background

I grew up in the Pacific Northwest, surrounded by coffee culture my entire life. I've always been drawn to the intersection of data and story: why do some products win on shelf while others sit? Why do some categories grow 40% while competitors stagnate?

Those questions led me to category management, then to product strategy, then to Nestlé — where I got to answer those questions at $200M scale.

What I Bring

I've operated at every stage, from scrappy DTC startups to global CPG conglomerates. That range is my edge. I know what a 6-figure brand needs differently from a $200M portfolio. I can build the infrastructure and move fast.

My approach combines sharp category analytics (Circana/IRI, Stackline, PowerBI) with a merchant's instinct for what actually moves product and an operator's discipline for making it repeatable.
