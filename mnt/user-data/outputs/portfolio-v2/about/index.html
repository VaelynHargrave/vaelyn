About

Vaelyn is a strategist, builder, and operator. She has a gift for connecting data to growth — and using it to transform brands and categories.

I recently led product commercialization at Nestlé — Starbucks & Nestlé Coffee Partners, managing a $200M CPG portfolio across mass, food, eCommerce, club, and value channels — including Walmart, Target, Costco, Sam's Club, and Amazon.

I launched the Starbucks Premium Instant Crema Collection in February 2025, the Seattle's Best Coffee brand re-launch, and drove a $1.2M assortment expansion at Sam's Club — all while building the infrastructure to make future launches faster and smarter.

Before Nestlé, I led category management at Crowd Cow, where I grew category margins by 35% YoY, managed 40,000+ SKUs, and rebuilt supplier onboarding from scratch. Before that, roles at Skin Addiction, Gift Horse Gems, Zulily, and Nordstrom gave me a full-stack view of eCommerce, retail, and brand building.

I've operated at every stage — from scrappy DTC startups to global CPG conglomerates. That range is my edge.
